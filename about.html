About Jack,

Jack is a technology enthusiast with an on-going love-hate relationship with Linux, but Windows always has his back. He's an IT worker by day and gamer at night with a kick for all things 80s inspired.

Education

Jack has completed a Certificate IV in System Analysis and Design, which along side his 4 year experience as a Systems Analyst has given him the critical skillsets to analyse problems in detail and negotiate to provide effective solutions.

Jack is also 90% through his Bachelor of Information Technology degree at The University of Canberra, majoring in Information Systems and Software Engineering, he also has opted to focus on Cyber Security and Networking subjects.

Virtualisation and Hardware Experience

Jack has had plenty of hands on experience with various virtualisation solutions, ranging from VMware, Hyper-V and KVM. These have also allowed for experience within the Linux area ranging between Ubuntu, RedHat and SuSE.

Jack also has plenty of experience with hardware, going from Smartphones, Laptops, Desktops to Servers. This includes knowledge of Server RAID installation and configurations, Network Card configurations, IPMI solutions, Hardware maintainence and replacements and, bare metal backups and restorations.

Windows Experience

Jack currently works with Windows 10 and Windows Server 2012 R2 and Server 2016. He has knowledge on planning and setting up Server and Desktop infrastructures, with recently working on Windows 10 Redstone 2 builds to enterprise and Windows Hello for Business both On-Premesis and Hybrid via Microsoft Azure.

Jack plans to become a Microsoft Certified Solutions Expert, giving further deeper skils within the Cloud Platform and Infrastructure areas.

Personal Hobbies

Jack enjoys in his own time to assist running a open source platform DeviateIT, giving open source projects free virtualisation solutions.

He also spends his time learning new technologies, programming languages, and maintains his own personal servers for development and learning. Jack runs as a personal website and blog as part of these hobbies The System is Down (a slight play on The Website is Down). He loves video games particularly realistic First Person Shooters, Flight Simulators and Space Flight Simulators in the rest of his spare time.
